Privacy Policy for Skrivbord

Last updated: July 21, 2026

Skrivbord is a macOS menu bar app for controlling Bluetooth standing desks (including the IKEA Idasen). This policy explains what data Skrivbord does — and does not — collect.

Summary

Skrivbord does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. It has no analytics, no crash reporting, no advertising, no user accounts, and makes no network requests of any kind.

Bluetooth

Skrivbord uses Bluetooth Low Energy to find, connect to, and control your standing desk. This communication happens entirely between your Mac and your desk over a direct local Bluetooth connection. No desk data (height, connection status, or otherwise) is sent anywhere else, stored remotely, or shared with Skrivbord's developer or any third party.

Data Stored on Your Device

Skrivbord stores a small amount of preference data locally on your Mac, using Apple's standard UserDefaults storage:

  • Your saved sit and stand height presets
  • The identifier of your desk's Bluetooth peripheral, so Skrivbord can reconnect automatically

This data never leaves your device. It is not backed up to iCloud, not transmitted over the network, and is deleted automatically if you uninstall the app.

Shortcuts and Automation

Skrivbord supports the Shortcuts app, Siri, and a custom URL scheme (skrivbord://) so you can trigger sit/stand commands from automations you create. These features operate entirely on-device and do not transmit any data externally.

Third Parties

Skrivbord does not integrate with, or share data with, any third-party service, SDK, or advertising network.

Children's Privacy

Skrivbord does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children, because it does not collect information from anyone at all.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, an updated version will be posted here with a revised "Last updated" date.
